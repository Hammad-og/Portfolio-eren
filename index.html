About me

One student,
many obsessions

I'm Mohammad Hammad, 18, studying at Syed Abid Husain Sr. Sec School, Jamia Millia Islamia. Finished Class 10 with 97%. That's the résumé part.

The real me is on a badminton court. It's not just a sport — it's the one place my head goes completely quiet. I've played soccer too, on dusty Delhi grounds, full commitment, zero regrets about the bruises.

Late nights go to building things — websites, bots, UI experiments, tools I wanted to exist. And somewhere in between, I fall into ancient art: old scripts, forgotten aesthetics, beauty that time couldn't erase.
